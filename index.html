About Jo

A practical musician with a sound-design ear.

Jo grew up between England, Singapore and Australia, then kept music at the centre of school, performance and university work. The through-line is curiosity: from choirs and bands to Foley, production, editing, mixing and experimental sound.

Early music

Piano lessons, recorder club, choir and a first school solo at age five.

Training

ABRSM grades in trumpet, piano and music theory, plus strong iGCSE and A level music results.

Performance

Orchestra, jazz band, audition choirs and a student band that won Battle of the Bands twice.

University

Music and Sound Design at Queen's University Belfast, with production, Foley and experimental audio.

Practice

The useful overlap.

Composition is treated as part of the whole audio system: motif, scene, edit, mix, voice, texture and timing.

Writing for picture Alternative scoring, temp score and motif development.
Recorded sound ADR, voice recording, Foley thinking and editing workflows.
Production tools Logic Pro X, Pro Tools, MIDI production, EQ, reverb and distortion.
Game audio Soundtrack and sound design for gameplay captures and interactive worlds.
