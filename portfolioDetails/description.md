# Jo Sockett Portfolio

This is the portfolio website for Jo Sockett, a UK-based composer, musician and sound designer working across screen scoring, game audio, ADR, Foley, live ensemble composition and electroacoustic sound.

The site is presented as **PortfoliJo** and gives visitors a direct route into Jo's showreel, selected project work, creative background, practical audio toolkit, CV and contact details. It positions Jo as a practical musician with a sound-design ear, combining composition, recording, editing, mixing and experimental production for screen, games and story-led projects.

## Portfolio Focus

- **Film and screen scoring:** Alternative scores for *Spirited Away* and *Edward Scissorhands*, written and produced in Logic Pro X using MIDI sounds.
- **ADR and sound design:** *Agent MX Z3RO*, including ADR recording and editing, temp score editing, sound design and Jo's voice work for Agent Sierra.
- **Game audio:** Soundtrack and sound design work for *Hollow Knight* and *Stardew Valley* playthroughs, using Logic Pro X, Pro Tools, sourced effects, editing and mix workflows.
- **Composition:** Live and concert-led work including *The Firefly*, *I Want to go Home* and *Captain Seahorse and the Voyage of Doom*.
- **Electroacoustic and sonic arts:** Experimental sound pieces including *Maybe Just One More* and *What do you Love?*, built from transformed recordings, voice material, EQ, reverb, distortion and Pro Tools editing.

## Featured Projects

1. **Spirited Away Alternative Score** - an alternative screen score produced in Logic Pro X with MIDI sounds.
2. **Edward Scissorhands Alternative Score** - a rewritten score for a scene from the 1990 film.
3. **Agent MX Z3RO** - ADR, temp score editing, sound design and voice performance work.
4. **The Firefly** - a flute, cello and piano trio inspired by minimalism and Steve Reich.
5. **I Want to go Home** - an atonal composition study exploring unconventional writing.
6. **Captain Seahorse and the Voyage of Doom** - a seven-movement orchestral programme piece connected by a five-note motif.
7. **Maybe Just One More** - a sonic arts piece made by transforming a wind chime recording.
8. **Hollow Knight Soundtrack and Sound Design** - game audio, editing and mixing for a gameplay sequence.
9. **Stardew Valley Soundtrack and Sound Design** - original soundtrack and sound design for gameplay.
10. **What do you Love?** - an electroacoustic work shaped from recorded answers to a single question.

## About Jo

Jo grew up between England, Singapore and Australia, keeping music at the centre of school, performance and university work. Her background includes piano, recorder, choir, ABRSM grades in trumpet, piano and theory, school orchestra and jazz band, audition choirs, band performance, and Music and Sound Design study at Queen's University Belfast.

## Technical And Creative Toolkit

Jo's work spans Logic Pro X, Pro Tools, MIDI production, screen composition, motif development, ADR, voice recording, Foley thinking, audio editing, game audio, electroacoustic composition, EQ, reverb, distortion and mix decisions for picture and interactive media.
