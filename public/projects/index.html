All projects

Film scoring Spirited Away Alternative Score An alternative score to Spirited Away, written and produced in Logic Pro X using MIDI sounds. Film scoring Edward Scissorhands Alternative Score A rewritten score for Edward Scissorhands, produced with Logic Pro X and MIDI sounds. Sound design Agent MX Z3RO ADR and Sound Design ADR recording and editing, temp score editing and sound design for Agent MX Z3RO, including Jo's voice work for Agent Sierra. Composition The Firefly - Flute, Cello and Piano Trio A flute, cello and piano trio inspired by minimalism and Steve Reich, exploring unusual time signatures and limited musical material. Composition I Want to go Home - Composition Study A piece focused on atonality and unconventional composing, built as a practical study in bending theory rules. Composition Captain Seahorse and the Voyage of Doom A seven-movement orchestral programme piece about a ship lost at sea and returning to safety, connected by a five-note motif. Sound design Maybe Just One More - Sonic Arts A single wind chime recording transformed with stretch plugins, EQ, reverb and distortion into a blurred, disorientating sound world. Game audio Hollow Knight Soundtrack and Sound Design Music, sound design, editing and mix work for a Hollow Knight playthrough, using Logic Pro X, Pro Tools and sourced effects. Game audio Stardew Valley Soundtrack and Sound Design Original soundtrack and sound design work for a Stardew Valley playthrough, edited and mixed in Pro Tools. Sound design What do you Love? - Electroacoustic Piece An electroacoustic piece created in Pro Tools from voice recordings of people answering one question: what do you love?
